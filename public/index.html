My Expertises

Here is a reflection of my experience through the projects I have undertaken at two different universities spanning a period of 5 years.

Education Experiences
2022-Present

Undergraduate (Okan Uni.)

At Okan University, I learned how to intervene in historical residential places and design public buildings with specific themes that consider the location. I was responsible for conceptualizing and implementing the functional requirements of the building.

2017-2021

Undergraduate (Damascus Uni.)

I studied at Damascus University for 3.5 years and gained skills in designing studios, schools, residential buildings, compounds, and public buildings. Additionally, I learned about structures and the use of wood, stone, metal, and concrete in construction.

2022-2023

Samatya Residence.

a residential building in Al-Fateh Samatya, Turkey, that preserved the historical significance of the area while incorporating modern interior design and adhering to government regulations.

2021

Conference Palace.

a conference palace in Damascus that blends traditional and modern elements, meets safety regulations, and represents the values of the city.

2021

Municipality Building.

The government building project on a sloped land overcame challenges such as stability, retaining walls, and drainage, and included accessibility and security features, resulting in a functional and secure building with a design that conveys authority.

2021

Modern Residential Urban Design.

The urban area design balances modernity with functionality, considering natural light and wind, while including essential elements such as residential buildings, bicycle roads, play areas, parking, and an interactive square, meeting the community’s needs

2020

Residential Urban Design.

A residential building was designed in a suburban area with separate living spaces for families, space for animals and parking, and sustainable feature.

2020

Single Residential.

a residential building project meeting strict government regulations and incorporating three squares of houses with duplexes on the top floor, resulting in a functional and visually appealing design.

2020

Damascus Uni. Gate.

an inviting and visually striking gate for an architectural university in Damascus that incorporated local culture and Islamic patterns, with decorative lighting and patterns that reflected the local architecture.
